    About Me.

    Hi there!

    I am Szani Lee, a passionate UX/UI designer, who was born and raised in Hungary, but currently happily residing in the Grand Canyon State. My never-quenching thirst for being able to create not just aesthetic, but problem solving products turned me into a designer eventually. I decided to become a UX/UI Designer as I truly enjoy the investigative aspects of research and design. I love being involved in the entire design process, having my voice matter and generating innovative solutions that results in digital products that are easy and enjoyable to use for people.
